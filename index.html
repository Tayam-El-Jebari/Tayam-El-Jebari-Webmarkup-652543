 Visit Ibiza, Spain

Ibiza has the most beautiful parks in Spain, a wonderful climate and a place on the UNESCO World Heritage List. You are also in the right place for a city trip to Ibiza. So book your plane ticket to Ibiza and enjoy the clear blue sea, the warm sun and the beautiful nature during the day! Above all, can visit whenever you want! Because of it's Mediterranean climate it is always wonderful to stay here. You can visit the wide sandy beaches of over 18 kilometers long all year round!

 Visit Athens, Greece

Fly to one of the oldest cities in the world, Athens. As a culture lover, you will be amazed during a city trip in Athens. Temples, ruins, cathedrals and arenas; you imagine yourself in another world. Above the Athenian plain towers her best-preserved piece, the Acropolis with its Parthenon. The temple and the view over the city give a crushing impression! It is therefore not surprising that the Acropolis is on the UNESCO World Heritage List.

 Visit Rome, Italy

It is without doubt one of the most beautiful cities in the world: Rome. Of course, a visit to the Colloseum and the microstate the Vatican is on the agenda. You'll be sweet for days in the Vatican alone! Another gem is the Pantheon, which is over 2000 years old. Not enough time to see the whole city? Stand with your back to the Trevi Fountain and toss a coin over your left shoulder with your right hand. According to the myth, one day you will return to Rome!
