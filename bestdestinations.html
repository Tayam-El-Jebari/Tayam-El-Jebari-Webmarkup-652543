 Destination 1: Visit Ibiza, Spain

Ibiza has the most beautiful parks in Spain, a wonderful climate and a place on the UNESCO World Heritage List. You are also in the right place for a city trip to Ibiza. So book your plane ticket to Ibiza and enjoy the clear blue sea, the warm sun and the beautiful nature during the day! Above all, can visit whenever you want! Because of it's Mediterranean climate it is always wonderful to stay here. You can visit the wide sandy beaches of over 18 kilometers long all year round!

 Destination 2: Athens, Greece

Fly to one of the oldest cities in the world, Athens. As a culture lover, you will be amazed during a city trip in Athens. Temples, ruins, cathedrals and arenas; you imagine yourself in another world. Above the Athenian plain towers her best-preserved piece, the Acropolis with its Parthenon. The temple and the view over the city give a crushing impression! It is therefore not surprising that the Acropolis is on the UNESCO World Heritage List.

 Destination 3: Rome, Italy

It is without doubt one of the most beautiful cities in the world: Rome. Of course, a visit to the Colloseum and the microstate the Vatican is on the agenda. You'll be sweet for days in the Vatican alone! Another gem is the Pantheon, which is over 2000 years old. Not enough time to see the whole city? Stand with your back to the Trevi Fountain and toss a coin over your left shoulder with your right hand. According to the myth, one day you will return to Rome!

 Destination 4: Alicante,Spain

Surrounded by hills, white beaches and on the Costa Blanca, lies sunny Alicante. Thanks to Spain's Mediterranean climate, you can spend most of the year on the white sandy beaches. A day without sand between your toes? How about a city trip to Alicante! In the historic part of El Barrio you walk through the streets with colorful houses. The view over the city, the harbor and the Mediterranean Sea is a fantastic memory!

 Destination 5: Edinburgh, Scotland

Nestled on hills is Scotland's enchanting capital: Edinburgh. Here you can't miss one street: Royal Mile. The main street takes you from the famous Edinburgh Castle to the magnificent Holyrood Palace. Along the way you walk through medieval courtyards with nice souvenir shops and cozy restaurants. Tip! Make sure to be at Edinburgh Castle by 1:00 PM. As a tradition, a cannonball is fired there every day, except on Sundays! You can also view the Scottish Crown Jewels and the Stone of Destiny here.

 Destination 6: Kayseri, Turkey

Take a city trip to Kayseri, which has been inhabited for six thousand years. It offers something for every holidaymaker. You will find many cultural attractions and culinary highlights here, such as charming merchant houses and modern boutiques. In addition, Kayseri is the perfect base to explore the beautiful, rugged interior of Turkey. Are you a mountain lover? Just 30 kilometers from your destination is the impressive 4,000 meter high Erciyes. Once a volcano, now the ski resort of Turkey. So, winter sports enthusiasts, pay attention: for beautiful, wide slopes you have to be in Kayseri.
